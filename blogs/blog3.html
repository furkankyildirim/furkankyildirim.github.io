Hello again after a long time…

September, 27, 2021

Hello everyone after a long time. After my last blog, I am here with a brand new application. My new app is called SUchedule! SUchedule allows Sabancı University students to create their programs with a user-friendly interface. With this application, I hope to facilitate the course registration process for Sabancı University students. With the support of A. Burak Ayaz, who developed the web side of the application, the application is live in the App Store and under review in the Google Play Store as of today. I developed the front interface of the application with React Native. I developed the service part of the application with Python Flask and deployed it on Github with Gunicorn at https://suchedule.herokuapp.com. You can find the service codes of the application at https://github.com/furkankyildirim/sucheduleService and the mobile codes at https://github.com/furkankyildirim/sucheduleApp. In order to experience the application, you can download the application on Google Play Store and App Store.
